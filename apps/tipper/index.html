Tipper

Calculate tips and split bills easily with Tipper! Tipper is an easy tip calculator for your iPhone or iPod Touch. Spin the dial and Tipper calculates tips for you. Split bills with your friends with one button. No numbers to type in! Tipper does the math for you with ease!

Features

  • Calculate tips with easy touch controls!
  • Split bills easily with your friends, rounded to the nearest dollar automatically!
